Behind the Project

The Mind & Vision
Behind ChefAI

ChefAI was born from a simple problem: too many people stare into their fridges every day with no idea what to cook. Om Sankhe set out to fix that — combining AI, voice technology, and thoughtful UX into one seamless cooking companion.

Built as a final year project for TY BSC IT, ChefAI integrates Anthropic Claude and Google Gemini for intelligent recipe generation, with Supabase powering the cloud backend — all under the expert guidance of mentor Komal Bhanushali.

🎓TY BSC IT — Final Year Major Project
🤖AI-first approach with Anthropic + Gemini APIs
🗃️Cloud-powered by Supabase — real-time database
🌟Mentored by Komal Bhanushali (Roll No. 26)
